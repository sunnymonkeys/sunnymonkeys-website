Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

Last updated: April 14, 2026

At Sunny Monkeys LLC ("we," "us," or "our"), we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit sunnymonkeys.com or engage with our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact information — your name, email address, and phone number when you reach out to us through our contact form or by email.
  • Project information — details you share with us related to your brand or design project.
  • Usage data — basic analytics about how visitors interact with our website (pages visited, time spent, general location). This data is anonymized and used only to improve the site.
  • Technical data — your IP address and browser user agent string, collected automatically for security and spam prevention purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide our design services
  • Send project updates, proposals, and deliverables
  • Improve our website and the quality of our services
  • Send occasional updates about Sunny Monkeys (only with your explicit consent)
  • Detect and prevent spam or fraudulent activity

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our website or conducting our business, provided they agree to keep this information confidential.

If you request a password reset or account action through our client portal, your IP address may be included in transactional emails for security purposes.

4. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience — for example, to remember your preferences or keep you logged in to the client portal. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect some functionality of the site. Our site does not use advertising or cross-site tracking cookies.

5. Embedded Content from Other Websites

Pages on this site may include embedded content (e.g. LinkedIn posts, videos, or social media widgets). Embedded content from other websites behaves in the same way as if you had visited those websites directly. These third-party services may collect data about you, use cookies, or monitor your interaction with that content.

6. Data Retention

Contact form submissions and related correspondence are retained for as long as necessary to fulfill your project or respond to your inquiry. We may retain records for administrative, legal, or security purposes where required. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time. To exercise these rights, please contact us at contact@sunnymonkeys.com. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

9. Text Messaging

All categories of personal data described in this policy exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent. This information will not be shared with any third parties.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
